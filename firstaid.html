Medical Emergencies

  • Heart attack: Call emergency, chew aspirin if advised, keep patient calm.
  • Severe bleeding: Apply direct pressure, elevate if possible, do not remove embedded objects.
  • Fracture: Immobilize, apply cold pack, avoid moving the limb.
  • Burns: Cool with running water 20 minutes, do not apply ointments, cover loosely.

Fire Safety

  • Evacuate immediately; crawl low under smoke.
  • Stop, drop, and roll if clothes catch fire.
  • Use a fire extinguisher only if safe: Pull, Aim, Squeeze, Sweep.

Accident Handling

  • Ensure scene safety; turn on hazard lights; set warning triangle.
  • Do not move injured persons unless there is immediate danger.
  • Collect key details: location, number of injured, hazards.

Disaster Safety

  • Earthquake: Drop, cover, hold on; stay away from windows.
  • Flood: Move to higher ground; avoid walking/driving through flood waters.
  • Storm: Shelter in interior room; have emergency kit ready.
